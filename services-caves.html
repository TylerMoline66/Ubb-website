Turn Your Basement into a Man Cave

Many Herriman homeowners who are thinking about basement remodeling have a man cave in mind. Creative Construction Solutions is known throughout Herriman and beyond as the go-to basement contractor for creating the most inviting man caves. This type of testosterone-heavy retreat is perfect for a variety of uses.

Sports Themed Man Cave

The focus of a sports-themed man cave is frequently a large-screen TV, suitable for watching any game — football, soccer, basketball, baseball, hockey, tennis, and even golf! While the acoustics for a man cave aren’t as important as they are for a basement home theater, it’s still good to take them into consideration during construction.

Once we build out your space and mount your screen and speakers, you can start planning the furnishings and décor. Recliners with cupholders or a sectional sofa is a must! Consider decorating the walls with jerseys or towels with your favorite team’s logo.

Bar-Themed Man Cave

Wouldn’t it be awesome to recreate the bar from Cheers right in your basement?! You may not have space for that, but Creative Construction Solutions can still build you an amazing wet bar for your man cave.

We can show you lots of style options for your bar, including L-shaped, straight and curved. Think about what type of theme you want your wet bar to have as well. Do you like a Western look? Modern? Sophisticated? We can incorporate under-bar lighting for an authentic look, or install neon lighting on the ceiling for a retro, citified look. We’ll install all the shelving you need, plus we can even add mirrors, a TV, and a chalkboard — your wish is our command.

Game Themed Man Cave

Video games are all the rage now, but you may want a man cave with classic games such as a pool table or dartboard. Or maybe you prefer foosball, air hockey, or skee ball. You could opt instead for some retro arcade games such as Pac-Man, Asteroids, or pinball machines.

A hardwood floor is perfect for this type of man cave, and we can install lighting that makes your man cave look just like Dave & Busters!

Music Themed Man Cave

Are you a music lover? It doesn’t matter whether you play music or just listen to it, you can turn your basement into a music-themed man cave. Décor has to include framed vinyl and album covers on the walls!

If you love to create music, Utah Basement Builders can build a platform for your drum kit or install extra outlets for equipment such as electric guitars and amplifiers. We can also install speakers on the walls as well as safe mounts for guitars.

If you love listening to music, we can build shelving for stereo equipment and storage for record albums, CDs, and more.

Man Cave Basement Contractor

You can also mix and match any of these ideas. Put a wet bar in your sports-themed man cave. Hang Led Zeppelin posters in your game room.

As you can see, the ideas for basement finishing are practically endless. Call us today to get started on your plans for a man cave.
