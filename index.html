How much does it cost to finish a basement?

The cost of finishing a basement
includes a variety of factors.

The cost of finishing your basement depends on a wide variety of factors, including: type of cabinetry, plumbing fixtures, flooring etc. Ryan or Brain will individually bid each job to make sure pricing is as accurate as possible. Once hired, we will periodically send out updated invoices to make sure everyone is well-informed. Utah basement builders will never add additional charges to the invoice unless the customer approves a change order and the price is agreed upon by both parties. Utah basement builders will not give you a surprise bill at the end of the job; our quote is our guarantee. See our cost calculator to get a “ballpark” estimate of what your project will cost. If the estimate is near your budget, click “request a quote” and we will contact you to set up a time to give you an exact quote.

Quality work at affordable prices

Here at Utah Basement Builders, we understand that everyone has a budget that must be respected. We are happy to work with any budget, it doesn't matter how big or small. By completing most of the work in- house and not hiring out for specialty trades, we are able to pass those savings onto you. Let us know what you budget is, and we will develop ideas and plans accordingly.
