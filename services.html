Services


Utah Basement Builders provide full-service basement remodeling and finishing for customers in Salt Lake City and throughout Utah County.

We are a locally owned and operated company that takes great pride in our work, delivering maximum quality and value along with exceptional customer service. We can assist you with any size or type project, from adding a single bathroom to completing a full basement finishing project.

Salt Lake City Basement Finishing Services

If you have an unfinished basement, the sky's the limit. We can design a layout and floor plan that incorporates all the amenities and features you have in mind. Whether you'd like to add bedrooms and bathrooms, a kitchen or bar, living space, or specialty rooms like a home theater or game room, we bring your vision to life.

Utah Basement Remodeling

If your basement is already finished, we can assist you with any type of remodeling or renovation you have in mind. We can install new basement flooring, cabinets, built-ins, and storage areas. We can also install or replace basement windows and stairs.

No matter what type of project you’re considering, we invite you to give us a call today or contact us online to learn more about our Utah and Salt Lake county basement remodeling and finishing contractor services. We offer free estimates and no-obligation consultations.

Basement Theatre Rooms
