Basement Remodeling in Utah

Basement remodeling and finishing projects let you add comfort to your home, increasing livable square footage and enhancing your property’s value.

Finishing your basement is also the most cost-effective way to add bedrooms, bathrooms, storage, living areas, and specialty rooms, such as game rooms and home theaters.

A basement renovation project is an investment in your family and your home. Don’t trust just anyone to handle this critical project — call on the experts at Utah Basement Builders.

Why Basement Remodeling & Finishing Makes Sense

Your basement offers the potential for doubling the livable square footage of your home — or at least increasing it significantly.
But unlike an addition, the foundation, ceiling, and walls are already in place. Utilities are all in place as well, making it quick, easy, and affordable to connect power, water and drain lines throughout the space. Construction time and cost are lower, and the potential for interrupting your life is minimal. Heating and cooling your basement is also more cost-effective, thanks to the impressive insulating qualities provided by the surrounding soils.

Finally, you have virtually unlimited options for how you want to layout and configure your space. Build bedrooms, closets, bathrooms, storage space, or whatever you and your family prefer. Or if you rather, create a large, open, and flexible space you can define with furnishings.

Basement Remodeling & Finishing Options

Although you have the freedom to layout your space however you prefer, consider some of the most popular options for your project. These options deliver the maximum for your investment, improving the value of your home as well as its comfort and convenience for your family.

Home theater rooms are more popular than ever, especially as we are spending more time at home with family and friends. You can have a true cinematic experience without ever going out. Likewise, game rooms — especially those that include a basement bar or kitchen — are a perfect choice for families who enjoy video games, board games, and other leisure activities.

If you’re outgrowing your home, consider adding bedrooms and bathrooms. You can add common areas and living room space or even a full guest suite, which is perfect for visitors, older kids or in-laws.

Whatever your layout of choice, we can create a design with the basement flooring, built-ins, and finishes you prefer. We can install basement windows to maximize natural light and safety and even replace your basement stairs with a safe and functional new staircase.
